Chorlton Bikes

Wins & Highlights – January 2026

Celebrating delivery, partnerships, and community impact.

Major Contract Success

  • NHS contract renewal secured, providing vital stability and recognition of our work. Huge thanks to Nick for leading this.

New Work & Partnerships

  • New delivery work with Long Boi's Bakery serving cafés in Didsbury and Withington.
  • Quoted £3,437.82 to deliver 12,781 brochures for the Chorlton Arts Festival (awaiting committee decision).
  • Strong engagement from Chorlton Civic Society, opening doors to future collaboration.
  • Exploring composting and employment pathways with M.U.D. / Brewcycle.

Community & Outreach

  • Community Ride 2026 planning underway with councillor engagement and a proposed February planning meeting.
  • Trishaw pilot training discussions with Stretford Public Hall.
  • Improved coordination and access with the St Margaret's trishaw group.

Bikes, Operations & People

  • 21 bikes moved to support Bikes4Refugees across January.
  • Cav bike currently being serviced at MCR Bikes; insurance claim now progressing.
  • Funding bid submitted to GMCC following its closure.

  • Heartfelt thanks to Ali S for years of riding and support.
  • Preparing for the King's Award Local Assessment Visit.

Thank you to everyone who makes this work possible. A strong start to the year for Chorlton Bikes.
