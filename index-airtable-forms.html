Who We Are

Chorlton Bikes (formerly Chorlton Bike Deliveries) is a community benefit society dedicated to providing sustainable, community-focused bike delivery services across South Manchester. We started as a grassroots response to COVID-19 and have grown into a thriving cooperative with a mission to reduce carbon emissions, support local communities, and create meaningful employment opportunities.

As we transition from a limited company to a community benefit society, we're excited to welcome new members and supporters who share our vision of a cleaner, more connected community.

Our Work

Commercial Deliveries

We provide green, reliable delivery services for businesses and public sector organisations, helping reduce their carbon footprint.

Community Deliveries

Supporting local organisations and groups with community-focused delivery services through our dedicated volunteer riders.

Trishaw Rides

Operating a Trishaw scheme across South Manchester, giving older people and those with limited mobility the opportunity to get out and about.

NHS Deliveries

Proud to keep vital services running with our ongoing contract with the NHS, delivering essential items sustainably.

Bike Library

Thanks to support from TfGM, we've launched a bike library to boost access to bikes for our community.

Recycling Project

Our ReCycle project collects reusable household items by e-cargobike, taking them to charity shops, homeless projects, and refugee organisations.

Friends of Chorlton Bikes

Become a member of Chorlton Bikes and join our community of supporters who believe in sustainable transport and community empowerment. As a member, you'll have a say in how we operate and help shape the future of sustainable deliveries in South Manchester.

Membership is open to anyone who shares our values and wants to support our mission. Join us and be part of the change!
